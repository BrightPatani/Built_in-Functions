Timers and Delays

Follow these steps to access the browser's console tab:

  1. Right-click anywhere on the page
  2. Select "Inspect" This will open the DevTools panel.
  3. In the DevTools panel, navigate to the "Console" tab.
  4. You'll see the console area where you can type and interact with JavaScript code.

Timeout Example

Click "Try it". Wait 4 seconds. The page will alert "Hello!... How are you?".

Click "Stop" to prevent the alert from being shown.

(You must click "Stop" before the 4 seconds are up.)
